CS360 – Computer Graphics Portfolio

by Anushka Singh | Instructor: Prof. Soumya Dutta, IIT Kanpur

Assignment 1: 2D Scene Rendering and Animation

Recreation of a 2D animated scene using basic primitives (square, triangle, circle) and affine transformations.

Assignment 2: Shading Models (Flat, Gouraud, Phong)

Three simultaneous viewports demonstrating per-face, per-vertex, and per-fragment shading with interactive light and zoom controls.

Assignment 3: Reflection and Texture Mapping

3D scene showcasing texture mapping, cubemap reflections, refractions, and a dynamic skybox environment.

Assignment 4: Shadow Mapping with Rasterization

Two-pass shadow mapping implementation with real-time light position control and camera orbit animation.

Assignment 5: Ray Tracing

Fragment-shader-based ray tracer rendering spheres with Phong lighting, shadows, and reflections.
